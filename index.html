    Instructions

    Welcome to the Cricket Team Splitter application! Follow these steps to create balanced cricket teams:

    1. Add Players: Enter the player's name in the text field and select their role from the dropdown menu. Available roles are Batter, Bowler, All-Rounder, and Wicket Keeper. Click the "Add Player" button to add them to the player list.
    2. Edit Players: In the player list, you can edit a player's name and assign them as Captain or Vice Captain by clicking the pencil icon (üñâ) next to their name. A modal will appear allowing you to enter a new name and select their position.
    3. Remove Players: To remove a player from the list, click the '√ó' icon next to their name.
    4. Teams Automatically Split: As you add, edit, or remove players, the teams will be automatically and equally split based on the players' roles to ensure balanced teams.
    5. Swap and Rearrange Players: You can drag and drop players between teams or within the same team to swap or rearrange them.
    6. Add Teams: If you want to create more than two teams, click the "Add Team" button. Players will be redistributed automatically among all teams.
    7. Shuffle Teams: If you want to redistribute players among the teams after they have been added, click the "Shuffle Teams" button. Players will be reshuffled and teams will be updated automatically.
    8. View Teams: The teams will be displayed in the "Teams" section. Each team shows the list of players and their roles.
    9. Download PDF: To save the team configurations, click the "Download PDF" button. A PDF file containing the team details will be downloaded.
    10. Reset: To start over, click the "Reset" button. This will clear all players and reset the teams.
    11. Dark Mode: Use the toggle switch at the top-right corner to switch between light and dark modes for better visibility.
