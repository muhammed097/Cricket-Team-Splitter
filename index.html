    Instructions

    Welcome to the Cricket Team Splitter application! Follow these steps to create balanced cricket teams:

    1. Add Players: Enter the player's name in the text field and select their role from the dropdown menu. Available roles are Batter, Bowler, All-Rounder, and Wicket Keeper. Click the "Add Player" button to add them to the player list.
    2. Edit Players: In the player list, you can edit a player's name by clicking the pencil icon (üñâ) next to their name. A prompt will appear allowing you to enter a new name.
    3. Remove Players: To remove a player from the list, click the '√ó' icon next to their name.
    4. Teams Automatically Split: As you add, edit, or remove players, the teams will be automatically and equally split based on the players' roles to ensure balanced teams.
    5. Add Teams: If you want to create more than two teams, click the "Add Team" button. Players will be redistributed automatically among all teams.
    6. View Teams: The teams will be displayed in the "Teams" section. Each team shows the list of players and their roles.
    7. Download PDF: To save the team configurations, click the "Download PDF" button. A PDF file containing the team details will be downloaded.
    8. Reset: To start over, click the "Reset" button. This will clear all players and reset the teams.
    9. Dark Mode: Use the toggle switch at the top-right corner to switch between light and dark modes for better visibility.
